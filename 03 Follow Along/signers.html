Who were the Signers?

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
State: Virginia
Principal author of the Declaration.
Later became the 3rd President of the United States.
John Adams
State: Massachusetts
A leading advocate for independence in Congress.
Later became the 2nd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
State: Pennsylvania
Famous diplomat, scientist, and inventor.
Served as ambassador to France during the Revolution.
John Hancock
State: Massachusetts
President of the Continental Congress.
Known for his large and bold signature on the Declaration.
Samuel Adams
State: Massachusetts
Leader in the movement for independence; cousin of John Adams.
Roger Sherman
State: Connecticut
Only person to sign all four major U.S. founding documents (Continental Association, Declaration, Articles of Confederation, and Constitution).
